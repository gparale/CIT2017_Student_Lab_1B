About Mr. Grimfield

Kasai Daichi Grimfield was born in cycle 13483481863881646878216786712(in human languange and meaning of date and time, in July 7th, 1858), in the location Sol:Terra:Z2960,34.6937 N, 135.5022 E (In a place called hell just under Osaka)

During his childhood, he spent most of his time trapped inside the underworld, riding a giant three-headed dog(called a cerberus),chasing away possessed skeletons. That is until a fateful day in 1943, Osaka was bombed, the city above the undergound ruined. However it opened a chasm leading to the underground.

His first glimpse in the surface world was a dark grim reality of what war is. However, it didn't really affect much about his personality. Still, he rode the cerberus to unknown lands, unbeknownst as to what he will see in this brave new frontier.

During his stay in the overworld, he managed to form a family group corresponding to the name "yakuza". This organization is responsbile for human trafficking, weapons smuggling, bribery and all the other criminal stuff.
